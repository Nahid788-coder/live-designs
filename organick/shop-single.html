Product Description

Broccoli is a green vegetable that vaguely resembles a miniature tree. It belongs to the plant species known as Brassica oleracea. It's closely related to cabbage, Brussels sprouts, kale, and cauliflower - all edible plants collectively referred to as cruciferous vegetables.

Our organic broccoli is grown on certified organic farms, ensuring you receive produce that's free from harmful chemicals. Each head is hand-picked at the perfect time to ensure optimal freshness and nutritional value.

Health Benefits:
  • Rich in vitamins C, K, and A
  • High in fiber and protein
  • Contains powerful antioxidants
  • Supports digestive health
  • May help reduce inflammation
