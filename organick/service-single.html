Organic Farming Services

Organic farming is an agricultural system that uses ecologically based pest controls and biological fertilizers derived largely from animal and plant wastes and nitrogen-fixing cover crops. At Organick, we've been practicing organic farming for over 12 years, refining our methods to produce the highest quality organic products.

Modern organic farming was developed as a response to the environmental harm caused by the use of chemical pesticides and synthetic fertilizers in conventional agriculture. Our methods promote ecological balance and conserve biodiversity while producing nutritious, safe food for our customers.

Our Specialty

We specialize in growing a wide variety of organic produce, including:

  • Fresh vegetables grown without synthetic pesticides
  • Organic fruits harvested at peak ripeness
  • Free-range eggs from pasture-raised hens
  • Raw honey from our own organic apiaries
  • Organic nuts and dried fruits
  • Seasonal specialty crops

Our Process

Our organic farming process follows strict guidelines to ensure the highest quality:

  1. Soil Preparation: We use compost and natural fertilizers to enrich the soil
  2. Seed Selection: Only certified organic, non-GMO seeds are used
  3. Natural Pest Control: Beneficial insects and companion planting protect our crops
  4. Hand Harvesting: Each item is carefully picked at optimal ripeness
  5. Quality Inspection: Rigorous checks ensure only the best reaches you
