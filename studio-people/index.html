Dedicated to Superior Studio Design

We pride ourselves on designing, specifying and constructing the best media facilities in the world - but what makes our facilities so good? Well, they don't just look good - they also consistently out-perform other facilities in terms of acoustics and isolation.

We design spaces that not only have design flare and work acoustically but also induce a perfect atmosphere for artists, performers, engineers, producers and teachers alike to do what they do best; be inspired and get creative. We undertake projects in every area of the media industry and there is no project too big or too small for us to handle.
